Site Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will promote small businesses in Pachuca Hidalgo, a small city next to Mexico City, about 40 minutes. It will provide general information about the city, businesses and any other information of importance for visitors, it will include upcoming events too. Pachuca is known for its national parks and places for camping.
