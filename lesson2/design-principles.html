Contrast

GoContact
https://www.gocontact.com/

This website demonstrates the contrast with the text in white and the background in blue. This makes easier to read the texts and it is easy to recognize what are the most important sections in the website.

White Space

Avaya
https://www.avaya.com/es/contacts/

The form for this website is well designed with proper white space, margins and padding between each form element. This design clearly help users to identify each one of the inputs that are required to complete with good readability and comprehension on intent.

Repetition

Amazon
https://www.amazon.com/

Amazon is a great example of repetition of content and elements. Each product section and items are the same along the website. This is helpful for the navigation, particulary for the phone usage. All of the website keeps the same navitation structure.
